Event Overview

Learn How to Build & Launch Your Startup Idea — Without Writing a Single Line of CodeTurn your ideas into real, functional digital products using Bubble — the leading no-code platform. This hands-on masterclass is designed to help creators, founders, and freelancers quickly build MVPs (Minimum Viable Products) that are scalable, testable, and investor-ready.

About Our Host

John Doe — No-Code Expert & Product Strategist  Andrew has launched over 50 digital products using Bubble for clients ranging from solo founders to startup accelerators. As a product strategist and educator, he’s known for breaking down technical concepts into simple, actionable steps. In this session, he’ll walk you through the entire MVP-building process — from idea validation to launch — using real examples and proven frameworks.

What You’ll Learn

No-code tools are software platforms designed to enable users to build digital products using visual interfaces instead of traditional programming. Examples include Webflow for website design, Bubble for app development, and Zapier for automating workflows. These tools abstract away the complexity of code, making development accessible to anyone with an idea and internet access.

Why it matters

  • What is an MVP and why it matters
  • How to turn your idea into a clickable prototype
  • Building with Bubble: UI, workflows, databases, and user logic
  • How to structure your app for future scalability
  • Launching your MVP and gathering user feedback
  • Tools and templates to accelerate your build process

  • User feedback survey template
  • List of tools to complement your Bubble app (e.g., email, payments, analytics)

Who Should Join?
